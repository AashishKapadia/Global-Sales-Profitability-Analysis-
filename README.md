# 🌍 Global Sales & Profitability Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **multi-regional revenue, profit margins, and channel efficiency** using a Kaggle dataset of **100,000 sales records across 185+ countries (2010–2017)**.  
The analysis was performed with **Python (Pandas)** for data cleaning, **Tableau Public** for visualization, and documented in **Microsoft Word**.

Key insights:
- Total Revenue: **$133.6 Billion**  
- Total Profit: **$39.4 Billion**  
- Overall Profit Margin: **29.5%**  
- **Sub-Saharan Africa & Europe** drive 50%+ of global profit.  
- **Cosmetics** is the most profitable product ($7.3B profit, 40.98% margin).  
- **Household & Office Supplies** lead in revenue (~$28B each).  
- **North America** is under-penetrated but shows strong margin growth (+4.11%).  
- Profit margins remained flat for 7 years (~29.75%).

📈 Conclusions
* Sub-Saharan Africa & Europe are the twin engines of profitability.

* North America is the biggest untapped growth opportunity.

* Cosmetics is the star product — highest profit margin and efficiency.

* Clothes has the highest margin (67.2%) but low revenue — hidden opportunity.

* Profit margins have remained flat for 7 years — structural improvement needed.

* Online and offline channels are balanced but both declined sharply in 2017.

---

✅ Recommendations
* Expand in North America to capture high-margin growth.

* Scale Cosmetics and Clothes — most efficient profit drivers.

* Improve structural profit margins beyond 29.75%.

* Investigate 2017 decline across all channels.

* Balance portfolio by reducing reliance on Household & Office Supplies.
